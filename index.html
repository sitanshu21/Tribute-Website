Shri Pradip Purohit

member of legislative assembly(2014-2019)

Sahaj Vidhyak

Pradip Purohit is an Indian politician. He was elected to Odisha legislative Assembly from padampur in the 2014 Odisha Legislative election as a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party(BJP). He has started his social life from the "Balco Hatao Gandhamardan Bachao" andolan and fight for kendupatra pluckers,labourers and seasonal workers for their legitimate right. He was the founder and coordinater of this andolan,later thousands of people from every villages of padampur sub-division joined and supported him and became a public figure. Local people calls him "kuna Dada" with their affection towards him. He is the only MLA from padampur assembly to honoured with "Srestha Vidhayak"(Best MLA) from the Chief-Minister of Odisha. He fights for the people of padampur in every aspect and a pure ground leader. Being an opposition leader many times in assembly, he raised the voice of people and about the development of western Odisha and pressurize the state government to work on the issues. He always fight for the right of farmers and kendupatra labourers. He is President of "Kendupatra Karmachari Sangha" where he fights for the bonus,government help for education and marriage for the kendupatra sramik and seasonal workers. He served 5 years as a people's MLA, That's the reason he is a "Sahaj Vidhayak".
